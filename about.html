About

Built to make Discord scheduling effortless

We created the Discord Timestamp Generator because coordinating time across a global community should never require mental math.

Our mission

Discord brings together millions of communities spread across every continent and timezone. Yet one of the most common frustrations remains surprisingly simple: telling people when something is happening. A single announcement like "raid starts at 9 PM" can be misread by dozens of members in other regions. Our mission is to eliminate that confusion with a tool that is fast, accurate and genuinely pleasant to use.

What the tool does

The Discord Timestamp Generator converts any date and time into a dynamic timestamp using Discord's native <t:UNIX:STYLE> syntax. You choose the moment and the display style; we calculate the exact Unix value and hand you a copy-ready code. Once pasted into Discord, that code automatically renders in each viewer's local timezone - no plugins, no bots and no manual updates.

  • Supports all seven official Discord timestamp styles.
  • Shows a live preview that mirrors a real Discord message.
  • Runs entirely in your browser for instant, private results.
  • Works beautifully on desktop, tablet and mobile.

Privacy first

We believe a small utility should not demand your data. The generator performs every calculation locally in your browser, which means the dates and times you enter never leave your device. There are no accounts, no tracking pixels harvesting your inputs and no server-side storage. You can read the full details on our Privacy Policy page.

Who it is for

Server owners, moderators, esports organizers, streamers, study groups and remote teams all rely on precise timing. Whether you are announcing a tournament, scheduling a community movie night or setting a project deadline, dynamic timestamps keep everyone on the same page - literally. If you have feedback or a feature request, we would love to hear from you on the Contact page.
