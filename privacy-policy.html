Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here is exactly how the Discord Timestamp Generator handles your information.

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the Discord Timestamp Generator ("we", "us", "the tool" or "the Service") handles information when you use this website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping the Service simple, transparent and safe to use.

2. Information we do not collect

The core generator runs entirely within your web browser. The dates, times and formats you enter are processed locally on your device and are never transmitted to, or stored on, any server controlled by us. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for personal information to use the tool.

3. The contact form

The contact form on this website is a front-end demonstration. It validates your input in the browser and confirms submission locally. In this demo configuration, the information you type is not transmitted to a server or retained. If this form is ever connected to an email or backend service in the future, this policy will be updated to describe how that information is handled.

4. Cookies and local storage

The Service does not set advertising or tracking cookies. Any use of browser storage, if introduced later, would be limited to remembering your preferences (such as a default timestamp format) and would remain on your device.

5. Third-party services

This website is designed to run without third-party trackers. If we add analytics or hosting services in the future, we will disclose them here along with links to their respective privacy policies. This tool is not affiliated with, sponsored by or endorsed by Discord Inc.

6. Children's privacy

The Service is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Data security

Because the tool processes your input locally and does not store it, the risk to your data is minimal. We nevertheless encourage you to keep your browser and operating system up to date to benefit from the latest security protections.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through our Contact page.
